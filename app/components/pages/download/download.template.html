Download MIBiG data

MIBiG data are available for download in several formats. The MIBiG annotations/metadata themselves are available in JSON format, while the sequences of the biosynthetic gene clusters are available in GBK format. A FASTA file with amino acid sequence translations of all genes from MIBiG entries is also available; this can, e.g., be used for BLAST searches to identify homologues of a query gene within any of the experimentally characterized gene clusters.
